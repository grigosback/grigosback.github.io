# Why keeping a public To-Do list?
- **First of all**, making it public creates a false sense of pressure in me, like I _have_ to finish these tasks, otherwise, people will see that I’m not making any progress. So it’s a way of pushing myself to get things done instead of sitting on the couch to watch another episode of The Punisher.

- **Secondly** (and perhaps more importantly), I think the best way to show the world what my interests are is by sharing what I do in my free time. It’s a good way to show **who** I am and **how** I am.
